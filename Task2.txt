justify-content Property:
Role: Determines how flex items are distributed along the main axis of the flex container.
Values:
flex-start: Items are packed toward the start of the flex container.
flex-end: Items are packed toward the end of the flex container.
center: Items are centered along the main axis.
space-between: Items are evenly distributed in the container with equal space between them.
space-around: Items are evenly distributed with equal space around them.
space-evenly: Items are evenly distributed with equal space around them, including before the first and after the last item.
align-items Property:
Role: Determines how flex items are aligned along the cross axis of the flex container (perpendicular to the main axis).
Values:
flex-start: Items are aligned to the start of the cross axis.
flex-end: Items are aligned to the end of the cross axis.
center: Items are centered along the cross axis.
baseline: Items are aligned based on their baselines.
stretch: Items are stretched to fill the container along the cross axis.
gap Property:
Role: Specifies the size of the gap between flex items. It's a shorthand property for row-gap and column-gap.
Value: A length value, percentage, or normal to specify the size of the gap.
flex-direction Property:
Role: Defines the main axis of the flex container, determining the direction in which flex items are placed.
Values:
row: Items are placed along the horizontal axis (from left to right).
row-reverse: Items are placed along the horizontal axis (from right to left).
column: Items are placed along the vertical axis (from top to bottom).
column-reverse: Items are placed along the vertical axis (from bottom to top).
flex-wrap Property:
Role: Specifies whether flex items are forced onto a single line or can be wrapped onto multiple lines within the flex container.
Values:
nowrap: All flex items are forced onto one line.
wrap: Flex items can wrap onto multiple lines as necessary.
wrap-reverse: Flex items wrap onto multiple lines in the opposite direction.
